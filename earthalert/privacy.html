Earth Alert

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Earth Alert ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Location data: Used to calculate distances to earthquakes. Not stored or shared.
  • Device ID / Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob for ad delivery optimization.
  • App usage data: Used by AdMob for ad performance measurement.

Earthquake Data

The App fetches real-time earthquake data from the USGS (United States Geological Survey) public API. No personal data is sent to USGS.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use device identifiers for personalized ads. You can opt out via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) on iOS or your device's ad settings on Android.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties other than Google AdMob as described above.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
